Easy Homemade Pasta

Description

Homemade pasta is a fresh and simple dish made from just a few basic ingredients: flour, eggs, and sometimes water or oil. The dough is kneaded until smooth, then rolled out thinly and cut into shapes like fettuccine, tagliatelle, or ravioli. Unlike store-bought pasta, homemade pasta has a tender, delicate texture and a rich, satisfying flavor.

Making pasta from scratch allows for endless creativity, as you can customize the shapes, flavors, and sauces to suit your preferences. Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Turn the dough out and knead it for 10 minutes.
  3. Let the dough rest.
  4. Divide the dough into balls.
  5. Use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into desired shapes.
